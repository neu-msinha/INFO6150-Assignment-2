about me

Introduction

I am an enthusiastic and results-driven software engineer with over three years of professional experience at SOTI India Pvt Ltd, a leading provider of mobile and IoT device management solutions. Throughout my career, I have consistently demonstrated my ability to contribute to all stages of the software development lifecycle, from designing and coding to testing and deploying applications. My primary focus has been full stack development, cloud engineering, and DevOps, areas in which I have successfully executed several key projects that enhanced the company’s technology stack and improved operational efficiency.


My Role in SOTI

At SOTI, I have worked extensively on the .NET technology stack, developing scalable, robust, and secure web applications. One of my key responsibilities has been API development, where I collaborated with cross-functional teams to design, build, and maintain RESTful APIs that seamlessly integrated with various front-end and back-end systems. In addition to my expertise in backend development, I also have hands-on experience in front-end technologies, including JavaScript frameworks such as Angular and React, allowing me to deliver full stack solutions that meet both the functional and aesthetic requirements of the end users.

My role at SOTI has also involved a strong focus on cloud engineering. I have worked on deploying applications and services on cloud platforms, leveraging tools like Microsoft Azure to ensure high availability, scalability, and performance. In the rapidly evolving field of cloud computing, I have constantly sought to stay up to date with new technologies and best practices, which has enabled me to implement cost-effective and reliable solutions. As a DevOps practitioner, I have been responsible for setting up continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD) pipelines, automating build and deployment processes, and maintaining infrastructure as code (IaC) using tools like Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes.


My Interests and Education

In addition to my technical expertise, I am a proactive problem-solver who enjoys tackling complex challenges and finding creative solutions. My collaborative approach to working with cross-functional teams, including product managers, designers, and QA engineers, has allowed me to deliver high-quality software that meets business requirements and exceeds customer expectations. My strong communication skills and ability to translate technical concepts into understandable terms have made me an effective team player and leader in various project settings.

Currently, I am pursuing a Master’s degree in Software Engineering at Northeastern University, Boston, with a focus on deepening my technical expertise and exploring new domains such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and advanced cloud architectures. I believe this academic endeavor will equip me with the skills and knowledge needed to tackle some of the most pressing challenges in the software industry, and to contribute to innovative solutions that drive technological advancement.

Looking ahead, I am excited to leverage my experience and education to contribute to impactful projects in software engineering, cloud technologies, and AI. My long-term goal is to continue growing as a software engineer while staying at the forefront of emerging technologies, enabling businesses to optimize their operations and create value through cutting-edge software solutions.
